**More and more people are spending large amounts of money on their appearance in order to look younger**

**Why? Is it a positive or negative development?**

**1. Opening** Viết lại đề và trả lời câu hỏi 1 cách ngắn gọn

An increasing number of individuals are paying more money in order to maintain their youthful appearance against aging. In this essay, I would evaluate the reasons behind this development before proposing whether it is beneficial or detrimental.

**2. Main**

**Paragraph 1:** Tại sao người ta càng ngày càng chi nhiều tiền để duy trì sự trẻ trung của mình.
- Line ý 1: Có yếu tố về mặt kinh tế - khi mà rất nhiều công việc yêu cầu rõ là cần ngoại hình. (hospitality / entertainment).
  - Vẻ ngoài không được tươi mới có thể bị "sidelined". 
- Line ý 2: Duy trì ngoại hình để cảm thấy tự tin về bản thân mình trong xã hội hiện đại, nơi mà ngoại hình được đánh giá cao.

The main driver is economic. In sales, hospitality, and entertainment, the job description explicitly includes the requirement for appearance, and older candidates may fear being sidelined. Since beauty treatments become more efficient and accessible, from using topical substances to lunchtime injectables, people can now regard instant revitalization of their appearance possible and necessary. 

On the positive side, evidence based routines, such as sunscreen or retinoids, are not simply for vanity purposes, but long-term health protection and a contributor to self-confidence. 

This trend is harmful since it normalizes ageism and pushes people towards debt-financed or poorly regulated procedures. The more society rewards performative youthfulness, the less space there is to value expertise and character, and the more waste we release into the environment in the forms of disposable packaging and fast-beauty churn.
